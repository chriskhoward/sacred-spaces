# FAQs 

**Q: Who is the Flow in Faith Sanctuary for?**

* The Flow in Faith Sanctuary is for Christians who want to nurture their relationship with God in ways that support the mind, body, and spirit together. It’s especially created with the lived experiences of People of Color at the center, offering a culturally aware, emotionally safe space where faith, embodiment, and healing can coexist without conflict.

   
**Q: Do I have to be a Christian to join?**

* You do not need to identify as Christian to join, but the Sanctuary is Christ-centered in its language, theology, and spiritual practices. Our teachings, prayers, and reflections are rooted in a liberated Christian faith and scripture, expressed through embodied practices like breath, movement, and stillness. We ask that all members enter the space with respect for that foundation.

**Q: Who is this membership NOT for?** 

* The Flow in Faith Sanctuary may not be the right fit if you’re looking for:  
  * A purely secular wellness or yoga membership  
  * A fast-paced, performance-driven program  
  * A space focused on productivity, hustle, or self-optimization  
  * A community that avoids faith or spiritual language altogether

This space is intentionally slow, reflective, and spiritually grounded. It’s designed for those who are ready to rest, soften, and tend to their inner life with care.

**Q: What does the Flow in Faith Sanctuary program offer?**

* The Sanctuary offers a consistent, supportive rhythm of embodied spiritual care, including:  
  * Bi-weekly live Christ-centered yoga and meditation classes  
  * A daily online community space for reflection, encouragement, and prayer  
  * Early access to YouTube yoga flows, meditations, and breath practices  
  * A monthly Community Circle for meditation, conversation, and prayer  
* Premium members also receive:  
  * A monthly themed devotional guide  
  * A monthly embodied practice bundle (flow, meditation, breath prayer)  
  * Free access to seasonal virtual retreats


Everything is designed to help you slow down, reconnect with God, and care for your whole self.

**Q: What kind of support can I expect as a member?**

* Support inside the Sanctuary is gentle, relational, and non-hierarchical. You can expect:  
  * Emotional and spiritual support through shared community space  
  * Guided practices that meet you where you are  
  * Encouragement without pressure or expectation  
  * Leadership that honors your autonomy, pace, and lived experience

This is not a space where you’re fixed, corrected, or rushed. It’s a space where you’re accompanied.

**Q: Are the LIVE Sessions Recorded?** 

* Yes. Live yoga and meditation classes are recorded and made available inside the Sanctuary so you can return to them or practice on your own schedule.  
  Monthly Community Circles are typically not recorded to preserve emotional safety and openness within the space.

**Q: Is there a commitment period for the membership?**

* No. There is no long-term contract or required commitment. You’re free to join, pause, or leave at any time. We trust you to stay as long as the Sanctuary is supportive for you.

**Q: What sets your membership program apart from others?**

* The Flow in Faith Sanctuary is unique because it intentionally brings together:  
  * Christian spirituality  
  * Emotional wellness  
  * Embodied practices  
  * Cultural care and safety

Unlike traditional church spaces that overlook the body, or wellness spaces that overlook faith, the Sanctuary honors both—without asking you to choose.

This is a space where rest is sacred, the body is trusted, and faith is lived gently.

**Q: How do I access the resources and materials included in the membership?**

* Once you join, you’ll receive access to the Sanctuary’s private online platform where all classes, recordings, community spaces, and resources live. Everything is accessible from any device, allowing you to engage in a way that fits your life, energy, and schedule.
